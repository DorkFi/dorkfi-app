## What is PreFi?

PreFi is a pre-launch deposit program that:

- **Rewards Early Supporters**: Earn a share of 5,000,000 VOI tokens
- **Non-Custodial**: Your deposits remain in your wallet
- **On-Chain Tracking**: All deposits are tracked on the blockchain
- **Time-Weighted Rewards**: Rewards are calculated based on amount √ó time until launch
- **Multiple Markets**: Support various tokens (VOI, aUSD, UNIT, BTC, ETH, ALGO, POW)

### Key Dates

- **Launch Date**: September 12, 2025 at 5:29 PM PDT
- **Phase 0 Duration**: From now until launch
- **Reward Distribution**: After mainnet launch

### Cross-Network System

PreFi operates across multiple networks:

- **Algorand Network**: Traditional Algorand-based tokens (ALGO, aUSD, etc.)
- **Voi Network**: VOI-based tokens and assets
- **Unified Rewards**: All networks compete for the same 5M VOI reward pool
- **Global Competition**: Your rewards depend on your performance relative to all users across all networks

## Understanding Rewards

### Reward Structure

- **Total Pool**: 5,000,000 VOI tokens
- **Distribution**: Based on stake-seconds (amount √ó time until launch)
- **Market Allocation**: Different markets receive different percentages of rewards

### Market Allocations

Rewards are distributed based on **actual USD deposit values** across all markets and **all networks**:

- **Cross-Network Allocation**: Rewards are allocated across both Algorand and Voi networks
- **Global USD Pool**: All deposits across all networks contribute to the total USD pool
- **Dynamic Allocation**: Each market's share of rewards is proportional to the total USD value deposited in that market globally
- **Real-time Calculation**: Allocations are calculated based on live deposit data from all networks
- **Fair Distribution**: Markets with more deposits receive proportionally more rewards
- **Open System**: No predetermined percentages - allocations emerge from user behavior across all networks

### How Market Allocations Work

1. **Total Deposits**: Sum of all USD deposits across all markets on all networks (Algorand + Voi)
2. **Market Share**: Each market's USD deposits √∑ total USD deposits across all networks
3. **Reward Allocation**: Market share √ó total VOI reward pool
4. **Dynamic Updates**: Allocations change as users deposit/withdraw on any network

### Example Allocation Calculation

If total deposits across all markets and networks are:
- VOI: $1,000,000 (40% of total)
- UNIT: $750,000 (30% of total)  
- BTC: $500,000 (20% of total)
- Others: $250,000 (10% of total)

Then reward allocations would be:
- VOI: 40% of 5M VOI = 2M VOI
- UNIT: 30% of 5M VOI = 1.5M VOI
- BTC: 20% of 5M VOI = 1M VOI
- Others: 10% of 5M VOI = 500K VOI

**Note**: These allocations are global across both Algorand and Voi networks.

### How Rewards Are Calculated

1. **Stake-Seconds**: Your deposit amount √ó seconds until launch
2. **Global Market Share**: Your stake-seconds vs. total market stake-seconds across all networks
3. **Reward Share**: Market allocation √ó your market share
4. **Final Reward**: Your share of the 5M VOI pool

**Cross-Network Competition**: Your rewards depend on your stake-seconds relative to all users across both Algorand and Voi networks, not just your current network.

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Wallet Connection Problems
- **Issue**: Wallet won't connect
- **Solution**: Refresh the page and try again
- **Alternative**: Try a different wallet

#### Transaction Failures
- **Issue**: Deposit/withdraw transaction fails
- **Solution**: Check your wallet balance and network fees
- **Alternative**: Try a smaller amount

#### Balance Not Updating
- **Issue**: Dashboard shows old balances
- **Solution**: Wait for blockchain confirmation
- **Alternative**: Refresh the page

#### Network Issues
- **Issue**: Wrong network selected
- **Solution**: Switch networks in your wallet
- **Alternative**: Use the network switcher in the header

### Getting Help
